Complete Watershed Delineation Workflow

DEM Processing, Flow Analysis, and Watershed Boundary Extraction

Workflow Overview

7-Step Watershed Delineation Process:

  1. Retrieve DEM from USGS 3DEP
  2. Fill sinks in elevation data
  3. Calculate flow direction (D8 algorithm)
  4. Calculate flow accumulation
  5. Extract stream network (threshold-based)
  6. Identify watershed outlet (pour point)
  7. Delineate watershed boundary
