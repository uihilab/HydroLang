Product Descriptions:

  • DEM: Raw elevation data from USGS 3DEP at 10m resolution
  • Slope: Rate and direction of maximum elevation change, critical for erosion and runoff modeling
  • Aspect: Compass direction of terrain orientation (0-360 degrees), used for solar radiation and vegetation analysis
  • TRI: Terrain Ruggedness Index quantifies topographic heterogeneity for habitat assessment
  • Hillshade: Analytical hillshade for cartographic visualization and terrain interpretation

Applications:

  • Watershed delineation and hydrological modeling
  • Erosion susceptibility mapping
  • Solar radiation and microclimate analysis
  • Wildlife habitat suitability modeling
  • Cartographic visualization and terrain interpretation
