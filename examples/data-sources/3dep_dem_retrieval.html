Terrain Analysis Products Generated:

  • DEM (Digital Elevation Model): Raw elevation data at 10m resolution
  • Slope: Rate of elevation change, useful for runoff analysis and erosion assessment
  • Aspect: Terrain orientation (compass direction), critical for solar radiation and vegetation studies
  • TRI (Terrain Ruggedness Index): Quantifies terrain heterogeneity for habitat modeling
