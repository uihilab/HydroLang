(static) AORC :Object

NOAA AORC (Analysis of Record for Calibration) datasource Provides access to high-resolution meteorological forcing data stored in Zarr format on S3 Supports multiple variables, time periods, and spatial regions

Data includes:

  • APCP: Total Precipitation (kg/m)
  • DLWRF: Downward Long-Wave Radiation Flux (W/m)
  • DSWRF: Downward Short-Wave Radiation Flux (W/m)
  • PRES: Pressure (Pa)
  • SPFH: Specific Humidity (kg/kg)
  • TMP: Temperature (K)
  • UGRD: U-Component of Wind (m/s)
  • VGRD: V-Component of Wind (m/s)
